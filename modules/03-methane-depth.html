FISCHER-TROPSCH PATHWAY STATUS
1
CO₂ feedstock availability

Atmospheric CO₂ (0.6 kPa) + subducted carbonates provide feedstock for synthesis.

CO₂ + 4H₂ → CH₄ + 2H₂O  (Fischer-Tropsch, 200–400°C)
2
Serpentinisation H₂ source

Fe-olivine + H₂O at 150–350°C produces H₂ required for CO₂ reduction.

3FeSiO₃ + H₂O → Fe₃O₄ + 3SiO₂ + H₂
